## Construction of low-density parity-check codes by superposition

- **Status**: ✅
- **Reason**: superposition 기반 구조적 바이너리 LDPC 구성법, girth>=6 보장하는 신규 코드 설계(E)
- **알고리즘 기여**: ✅ 알고리즘/코드 기여
- **ID**: ieee:1402646
- **Type**: journal
- **Published**: Feb. 2005
- **Authors**: Jun Xu, Lei Chen, Lingqi Zeng +2
- **PDF**: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/1402646
- **Abstract**: This paper presents a superposition method for constructing low-density parity-check (LDPC) codes. Several classes of structured LDPC codes are constructed. Codes in these classes perform well with iterative decoding, and their Tanner graphs have girth at least six.

## Shuffled iterative decoding

- **Status**: ✅
- **Reason**: shuffled 반복디코딩 스케줄링은 LDPC BP에 그대로 적용되어 수렴 가속하는 이식 가능 디코더 개선(C)
- **알고리즘 기여**: ✅ 알고리즘/코드 기여
- **ID**: ieee:1402639
- **Type**: journal
- **Published**: Feb. 2005
- **Authors**: Juntan Zhang, M.P.C. Fossorier
- **PDF**: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/1402639
- **Abstract**: Shuffled versions of iterative decoding of low-density parity-check codes and turbo codes are presented. The proposed schemes have about the same computational complexity as the standard versions, and converge faster. Simulations show that the new schedules offer better performance/complexity tradeoffs, especially when the maximum number of iterations has to remain small.

## Codes on finite geometries

- **Status**: ✅
- **Reason**: 유한기하 기반 QC-LDPC 구성법 신규 제시, 4-사이클 많아도 반복디코딩 잘 되는 코드 설계(E)이며 바이너리
- **알고리즘 기여**: ✅ 알고리즘/코드 기여
- **ID**: ieee:1386528
- **Type**: journal
- **Published**: Feb. 2005
- **Authors**: Heng Tang, Jun Xu, S. Lin +1
- **PDF**: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/1386528
- **Abstract**: New algebraic methods for constructing codes based on hyperplanes of two different dimensions in finite geometries are presented. The new construction methods result in a class of multistep majority-logic decodable codes and three classes of low-density parity-check (LDPC) codes. Decoding methods for the class of majority-logic decodable codes, and a class of codes that perform well with iterative decoding in spite of having many cycles of length 4 in their Tanner graphs, are presented. Most of the codes constructed can be either put in cyclic or quasi-cyclic form and hence their encoding can be implemented with linear shift registers.

## A Decoding Algorithm for Finite-Geometry LDPC Codes

- **Status**: ✅
- **Reason**: 유한기하 LDPC용 저복잡도 비트플리핑 디코더 알고리즘(C) - 바이너리, NAND BP 디코더에 이식 가능
- **알고리즘 기여**: ✅ 알고리즘/코드 기여
- **ID**: ieee:1402664
- **Type**: journal
- **Published**: Feb. 2005
- **Authors**: 
- **PDF**: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/1402664
- **Abstract**: We develop a new low-complexity algorithm to decode low-density parity-check (LDPC) codes. The developments are oriented specifically toward low cost, yet effective, decoding of (high-rate) finite-geometry LDPC codes. The decoding procedure iteratively updates the hard-decision received vector in search of a valid codeword in the vector space. Only one bit is changed in each iteration, and the bit-selection criterion combines the number of failed checks and the reliability of the received bits. Prior knowledge of the signal amplitude and noise power is not required. An optional mechanism to avoid infinite loops in the search is also proposed. Our studies show that the algorithm achieves an appealing tradeoff between performance and complexity for finite-geometry LDPC codes.
