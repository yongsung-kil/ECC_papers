A Measure of the Girth Histogram for Improved Girth-Conditioning Construction of LDPC Codes
Hua Chen and Zhigang Cao, Senior Member, IEEE

Abstract— In this letter, we present a general quantitative measure of the girth histogram of low-density parity-check (LDPC) codes with joint consideration of the degrees and local girths of symbol nodes. We then propose a further girth-conditioning algorithm to optimize such a measure during the construction of LDPC codes. We show by simulation results that the algorithm improves not only the performance of irregular codes at high signal-to-noise ratios (SNR) considerably, but also the rates of regular codes.

V. CONCLUSION
A general quantitative measure of the girth histogram of LDPC codes with joint consideration of degrees and local girths of symbol nodes was proposed, as well as an algorithm to construct further girth-conditioned codes by optimizing such a measure. Simulation results showed that the proposed algorithm not only improves the performance of irregular codes at high SNR values considerably, but also the rates of regular codes.
Finally it should be noted that there is an improved PEG algorithm [8] that has more or less the same performance as our proposed algorithm. However, the basic ideas are different in that to increase the size of small trapping sets, while the algorithm in [8] focuses on the connectivity of short cycles, our proposed algorithm improves the girth histogram by optimizing the proposed measure (1). Furthermore, improved girth histogram enables our algorithm to yield high-rate codes, which cannot be achieved by [8].
