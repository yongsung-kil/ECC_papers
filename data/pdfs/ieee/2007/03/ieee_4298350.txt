V. CONCLUSIONS
Through the sections 1-11, we provide mathematical models and exemplary algorithms of routines of the separated MLDA. In III, we estimate the complexity of the separated MLDA. In IV, we present the simulation results of the MLDA in terms of performances and computational complexities.

The authors of the paper also applied the MLDA to decoding
of LT codes, and presented the simulation results in [11]. In
that paper, the LT degree design with a small dense fraction
of rows is also discussed.
As an application of the MLDA scheme with LDPC and
LT codes, the authors are investigating the possibilities for
video transmission over BEC in real-time with block lengths
n within few thousands.
