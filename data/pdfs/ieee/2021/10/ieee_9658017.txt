Abstract—In order to meet the reliability requirements of 6G mobile communication, this paper proposes a construction method of spatially coupled low density parity check codes based on PEG algorithm. A new coupling algorithm is designed. Without changing the structure of the matrix, the coupling matrix is redesigned and the number of coupling edges is added. Simulation results show that this design has better decoding performance than 5G-NR-LDPC in binary input additive white Gaussian noise channel.
Index Terms—SC-LDPC codes; PEG algorithm; Coupling algorithm; Changed degree
I. INTRODUCTION
As one of the important transmission means of physical layer mobile communication technology, channel coding is of great significance to improve the reliability of the channel. As early as June 2015, the International Telecommunication Union explained the application scenarios of 5G mobile communication technology, including eMBB, mMTC and URLLC. In 2016 3GPP TSG RAN WGI conference, in order to solve the problem of emBB in mobile communication channel, LDPC code was determined as the coding standard of 5G mobile channel. Low-density parity-check (LDPC) codes are an important type of error-correcting code. They were proposed by Gallager in the early 1960s. LDPC codes are the most promising type of linear codes due to their capacity performance and low decoding complexity. [1], [2].
SC-LDPC codes are derived from LDPC convolutional codes. In 1999, Feltstrom and Zigangirov proposed LDPC convolutional codes. SC-LDPC codes are constructed by cou-

pling a series of disjoint or uncoupled Tanner graphs into a single coupling chain, which can be regarded as a special form of LDPC convolutional codes. In 2011, Kudekar and
other scholars proposed the concept of SC-LDPC code. The results show that SC-LDPC codes have good iterative decoding threshold and asymptotic minimum distance. It is proved that SC-LDPC codes have threshold saturation in binary erasure channel and binary memoryless symmetric channel.
In this letter, we give the basic construction method of
LDPC and couple LDPC through a large-scale screening method. Without changing the structure of check matrix, a certain number of base matrices of SC-LDPC are constructed
by the designed constraints, and the condition codes are obtained by eliminating the short loops, filling the cyclic
shift coefficients and extending CPM Check matrix of rate. We test the performance of the constructed SC-LDPC codes in Gaussian channel. The numerical results show that the
performance of the proposed construction method is better
than that of the 5G-NR-LDPC codes with bit error rate and frame error rate.
With the rapid development of communication technology, the theoretical research and application of SC-LDPC code have achieved good results. The current research mainly focuses on the design and construction of SC-LDPC code, decoding algorithm, performance analysis and application in various fields.

II. BASE CONSTRUCTION METHOD
The section introduces the basic method of randomly constructing LDPC matrix throughout the text.
Progressive Edge-Growth (PEG) algorithm is a very effective method to design large girth matrix based on computer. Its basic idea is to add an edge from the variable node to the check node each time, and ensure that the local girth of the current variable node is as large as possible. The initial input factors of PEG algorithm are the number of variable nodes , the number of check nodes , and the degree distribution of each variable node. Under these parameters, PEG algorithm adds an edge to each variable node and check node according to the rules, and constructs Tanner graph to maximize the local ring length. [3].
In general, the PEG algorithm starts from the smallest degree variable node and adds edges between the variable node and the check node. [5]. Until the degree of the current variable node is satisfied, the algorithm will move to the next .When all the variable nodes complete the edge growth, the Tanner graph is obtained by satisfying the given degree sequence. Fig. 1 shows an example of a Dy = {2, 2, 2,3, 3,3} tanner graph, in which the check degree sequence is uniformly of degree 3, ie., De = {8, 3, 3, 3}.

V. CONCLUSIONS
This paper proposes a construction method of improved SCLDPC codes based on PEG algorithm is proposed. The peg algorithm is used to construct the base matrix, and a new coupling algorithm is designed to change the check structure of the check matrix, eliminate the loop of the constructed base matrix and generate the coupling matrix. The cyclic permutation matrix algorithm is used to eliminate the influence of four cycles and reduce six cycles at the same time. The SCLDPC code which can meet the target code length and rate
