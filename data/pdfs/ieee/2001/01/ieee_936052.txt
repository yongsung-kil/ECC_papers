Abstract - In this paper, we propose a normalized BP-based algorithm which utilizes normalization to improve the accuracy of the soft values delivered by a previously proposed simplified BP-based algorithm, and can achieve an error performance very close to that of BP. We also extend the principle of normalization to improve the performance of the Max-LogMAP algorithm in turbo decoding.
I. NORMALIZEDBP-BASEDALGORITHM
The hardware implementation of belief propagation (BP) algorithm for LDPC codes [l]is limited by its complexity. In [2,3],a reduced complexity iterative decoding algorithm based on the BP algorithm has been proposed. It is referred to as UMP BP-based algorithm. Unfortunately, the UMP BPbased algorithm does not work very well for codes with check sums of large weights such as one-step majority logic decodable (OSMLD) codes.
