Abstract— For irregular LDPC codes,the density evolution method can track the messages to find out the noise threshold, enabling optimization of the degree distribution.A new optimized scheme for irregular LDPC codes is proposed in this paper, which is combining Zhengya He’s modified Particle Swarm Optimization(PSO) algorithm with discrete density evolution(DDE) for the offset BP-based decoding algorithm. Several optimized degree distribution pairs and corresponding threshold of LDPC codes over rician channel were given using the proposed scheme.The performance of the optimized LDPC codes was also analyzed. Simulation results shows that the proposed scheme is effective.
Keywords - irregular LDPC codes; discrete density evolution; Particle Swarm Optimization; offset BP-based decoding
I. INTRODUCTION
Low-density parity check (LDPC) codes, proposed by Gallager[1]in the 1960’s,and later rediscovered by Mackay et.al[2]. Luby[3] designed some irregular LDPC codes whose performance is very close to the Shannon limit on the erasure channel. Sae-Young Chung[4] and Jilei Hou [5] have optimized the codes on AWGN channel and Rayleigh fading channel. T.J.Richardson[6] developed a numerical technique, called density evolution ,which is a powerful tool to analyze performance of decoding algorithm and determine the thresholds to any desired degree of accuracy.
Jinghu Chen proposed the discrete density evolution (DDE) method based on offset BP-based decoding algorithm, it was applied on regular LDPC codes over AWGN channel [7].
The code optimization of irregular LDPC codes is a nonlinear cost function minization problem. Variables to be optimized are the coefficient of the degree distribution polynomial of irregular LDPC codes, i.e. the degree
distribution pairs (λ, ρ) of irregular LDPC codes.
PSO(Particle Swarm Optimization)is a evolution method proposed by Kennedy and eberhart[8]. It can obtain good performance in optimization problem. In this paper, we take Zhengya He’s modified Particle Swarm Optimization algorithm (HE_PSO) [9] to optimize the irregular LDPC codes on rician channel.
The paper is organized as follows: In section II, the preliminary theory of LDPC codes and rician channel are briefly reviewed. Then we present HE_PSO algorithm and

DDE(discrete density evolution) method applied on the optimization of degree distribution pairs over rician channel in Section III. Furthermore the simulation results are given in Section IV. Section V concludes the paper.
II. PRELIMINARY THEORY

A. Threshold effect of LDPC codes
LDPC codes exhibit an important property[5]: noise threshold effect, if the noise level of the channel is smaller than a certain noise threshold ,the bit error probability of any code in the ensemble goes to zero as the block size goes to infinity;if the noise level is above the noise threshold,the probability of error is always bounded away from zero.
The noise threshold is an important parameter to evaluate the performance of the LDPC codes. Optimization of irregular LDPC codes is to find the largest possible noise threshold for a given channel. The noise threshold of each LDPC ensemble defined by its degree distribution pairs could be obtained by DDE algorithm.

V. CONCLUSIONS
Excellent performance of the irregular LDPC codes depends on good degree distribution pairs. Discrete density evolution is a powerful tool to determine the noise threshold of LDPC codes. Zhengya He’s modified Particle Swarm Optimization algorithm is a efficient method for optimization. In this paper, we have proposed a new scheme for combining them to optimize the degree distribution pairs over rician channel. The optimized degree distribution pairs and corresponding noise threshold are given, error performance of the given degree distribution pairs is analyzed finally. It is desirable to optimize the irregular LDPC codes in this way.
