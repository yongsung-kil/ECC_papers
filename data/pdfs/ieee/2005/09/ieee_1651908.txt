and a i -rotation code with rate 0.75. These codes show

These figures show that IR-LDPC codes constructed through our method perform well. In addition, we present
the simulation data in TABLE I . We can observe that the
performance of these codes approach the Shannon Limits asymptotically as the block length tends to infinity or the rate
tends to minimum..
VI. CONCLUSION In this paper, we have proposed a new approach for the construction of IR-LDPC codes. Our approach is based on
the construction of V-Matrices, in which only cyclically shifting and linear matrix operations are required. Therefore, the complexity of code construction is efficiently reduced. Computer simulation results show that the generated codes have good performance.
Future work involves the graphical configurations and further improvement of the performance.
