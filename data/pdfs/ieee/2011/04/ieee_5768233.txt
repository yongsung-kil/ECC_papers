A Time-saving Algorithm for Constructing

QC-LDPC Codes Based on PEG Algorithm

Northeastern University Shenyang, China

Huang Liqun Department of Electronic Information, Northeastern University at Qinhuangdao

Abstract—We propose a new algorithm based on PEG algorithm to construct high-girth quasi-cyclic low-density parity check (QC-LDPC) codes. Before our improved algorithm in introduction,we depict the general algorithm based on PEG algorithm to construct QC - LDPC code ,if necessary to eliminate the short cycles, that would bring in cycles-search.When code length of basic matrix is not too long,the algorithm is feasible,but with the code length growth, cycles-search will bring large of calculation and time-consuming sharply. Therefore we put forward an improved method by using the specific relation of basic matrix rows to replace traditional cycles-search, effectively eliminates short cycles, and largely reduces the time complexity at the same time. Through the simulation analysis, the performance can also be comparable with random constructed LDPC codes.
Keywords-PEG; QC-LDPC; optimization
I. INTRODUCTION
Low-density parity-check (LDPC) codes were originally proposed by Gallager [1] in 1962, and proved its distance characteristics. It rediscovered by Mackay and Neal [2] in 1996. Similar to Turbo codes, LDPC codes are also a class of Shannon limit approaching codes when decoding with softdecision belief propagation algorithms such as the sum-product algorithm. At present, Two broad classes of methods have emerged for the construction of LDPC codes . One set of methods are based on random constructions, the second on algebraic constructions.PEG algorithm[4] is currently accepted as a very effective random constructing algroithm for the construction of the medium and short LDPC codes, it resorts to the way of adding edges in turn of the Tanner graph for the codes. In a given degree distribution situation, it makes the counts of the short cycles in the Tanner graph of the codes as little as possible, in order to achieve matrix with the local optimum. However, some codes will be hard to encode such LDPC codes efficiently because of the unfixed structure, especially for long codes, the amount of calculation for its construction and encoding is rising dramatically when completely using this algorithm to randomly construct.QCLDPC codes[10]rely on their own quasi circulation structure, need less storage space, and quasi circulation structure can effectively break short cycles, improve girth[2], which is very important factor for the performance of the codes,so that effectively resolves the above problems. Reference[5] gives a method that based on FEG algorithm to construct QC-LDPC codes, but partly eliminates short cycles, no expands girth.

Some other approaches that adopt random ways to construct basic matrix as the foundation, search all short cycles, and abolish them, although performance met requirements, but in the searching process, that will bring large amount of calculation. This article introduces the concept of rows relation matrix, to avoid large amount of calculation for cycles-search, achieves the purpose that enlarge the girth similarly.
The structure of this paper is as follows, Section II introduce the definition of QC-LDPC codes. The progressed construction method of QC-LDPC codes based on PEG algorithm will be introduced in Section III. Simulation results are presented in Section IV. Finally, Section V is devoted to our conclusions.

VI. CONCLUSION This paper proposed an improved construction method of QC-LDPC codes based on the PEG algorithm. The introduced algorithm,which can eliminate short cycles efficiently and saving lots of time, has almost the same performance with the random PEG algorithm. Furthermore, the introduced codes are easy to implement on hardware because of its quasi-cyclic structuren, and the parameters of the codes are flexible to be adjusted. Therefore, the QC-LDPC codes constructed by our algorithm are more preferable in applications.
