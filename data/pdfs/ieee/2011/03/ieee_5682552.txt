(рқ‘ҡ, рқ‘һ) = (5, 67), and (рқ‘ҡ, рқ‘һ) = (30, 79). The structure of
the eight-element stopping sets in рқҗ»(рқ‘ҡвүҘ4, рқ‘һвүҘ7) is

III. THE EIGHT-ELEMENT STOPPING SETS IN рқҗ»(рқ‘ҡвүҘ4, рқ‘һ)
In this section we determine the structure of the eight-
element stopping sets of рқҗ»(рқ‘ҡвүҘ4, рқ‘һ). Some of the results
are given in Theorem 2: the eight-element stopping sets of
рқҗ»рқ‘ғ(4, 7) are precisely the support of the minimum weight
codewords in рқ’һрқ‘ғ(4, 7), and there are exactly 147 eight-element
stopping sets for рқҗ»рқ‘ғ(4, 7). This theorem also states that
рқ‘ (рқҗ»рқҗј(4, 7)) = 9 and рқ‘‘(рқ’һрқҗј(4, 7)) = 10.
A length-рқ‘ӣvector v over a set рқҗҙis said to form a pattern
(рқ‘Ҙ1, рқ‘Ҙ2, вӢ…вӢ…вӢ…, рқ‘Ҙрқ‘Ў) if the components of v consist of precisely рқ‘Ў
distinct elements рқ‘Ј1, рқ‘Ј2, вӢ…вӢ…вӢ…, рқ‘Јрқ‘Ў, and рқ‘Јрқ‘–is repeated рқ‘Ҙрқ‘–times,
so that рқ‘Ҙ1 + рқ‘Ҙ2 + вӢ…вӢ…вӢ…+ рқ‘Ҙрқ‘Ў= рқ‘ӣ. As an example the vector
(2, 2, 1, 3, 3, 4, 4, 1, 3, 4) has pattern (2, 2, 3, 3). For simplicity,
we give an increasing order to the elements of a given pattern;
this ordering does not affect the statements and arguments.
The fact that the Tanner graph of рқҗ»(рқ‘ҡ, рқ‘һ) has girth six
implies that the row-patterns of any eight-element stopping set
of рқҗ»(рқ‘ҡ, рқ‘һ) are limited to the cases (4,4), (2,2,4), (2,3,3) and
(2,2,2,2). In the following lemmas we investigate these cases
for рқҗ»(рқ‘ҡвүҘ4, рқ‘һ). The results show that the only possible case
for the row-pattern of any matrix рқ‘Җrepresenting an eight-

VI. CONCLUSIONS
Two classes of array codes referred to as proper and
improper codes were considered in this paper. In two cases, the
improper codes were shown to be better than the proper ones
from the minimum distance and stopping distance perspective,
implying that the improper codes may have better performance
over both AWGN and binary erasure channels. Simulation
results were presented which show that the codes of lengths
2209, 3481, 4489 and 6241 confirm this claim. Accordingly,
from an application perspective, the improper array codes are
preferable to the proper array codes. In this respect, it was
also shown that the improper array codes compare well with
random-like codes.
