Design QC-LDPC coder based on FPGA

Abstract—In this paper we study some fundamental properties of QC-LDPC code, we use a recursive compression algorithm to optimize the structure of QC-LDPC code, it can effectively reduce the coding complexity, save the space which is used to storage QC-LDPC code, and don’t need to reduce its error correction performance. We use VHDL language to design QC-LDPC decoder in the FPGA chip. This system was simulated in Quartus Τ7.2.
Keywords-QC-LDPC code; recursive compression algorithm; FPGA
