III. GOOD SEQUENCES TO GENERATE SC-LDPC CODES

Here, we propose an explicit method to generate the compo-

nent matrices such that the final SC-LDPC codes are 4-cycle

free. In fact, the component matrices are constructed by some

new well-defined good sequences which are generated by a

depth first algorithm. The outputs show that the final SC-LDPC

codes in most cases match the minimum bound latency w

reported in Lemma 4 of [6].
