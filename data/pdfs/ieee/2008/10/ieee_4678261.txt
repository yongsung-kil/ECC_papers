Construction of Quasi Cyclic LDPC Codes Based on GirthOpt-PSO Algorithm

XU Hua
College of Physics and Electronic Engineering Yancheng Teachers University
Yancheng, 224002,Jiangsu, P.R.China jhxuhua@yahoo.com.cn

Abstract— The QC(quasi-cyclic) LDPC codes are an important subclass of LDPC codes. The key to improving the performance of QC LDPC codes is how to construct a parity-check matrix H with a girth distribution as good as possible. In this paper, method of girth detection of QC LDPC codes is described first .Furthermore ,a novel algorithm , GirthOpt-PSO algorithm, is proposed to construct the QC LDPC codes, which achieves a good girth distribution based on the PSO algorithm. Simulation results show that the performance of the QC LDPC codes constructed by proposed algorithm is better than Tanner code and Array codes .Besides, the proposed algorithm is more flexible for designing the QC LDPC codes with desired block length and rate as well as good girth.
Keywords- QC LDPC codes, Girth distribution, Particle Swarm Optimization
I. INTRODUCTION
Low-density parity check (LDPC) codes, proposed by Gallager in 1962, were rediscovered by Mackay in 1990’s. Ever since their construction, decoding, efficient encoding and applications in digital communication have become focal points of research.
Methods for constructing LDPC codes can be classified into two categories:one is random or pseudorandom constructions, the other is algebraic constructions[1]. Long random LDPC codes in general perform closer to the Shannon limit than their equivalent algebraic constructed LDPC codes, however, they usually do not have sufficient structure to allow simple encoding.The encoding problem may be an obstacle for their commercial application. The other disadvantage is that the general LDPC codes need amount of memory to store the parity check matrix.
QC LDPC codes may be a good candidate for solving the memory and encoding problem of LDPC codes for their parity check matrix consists of circulant permutation matrix or zero matrix. Tanner code and array code are two representive QC LDPC codes[2,3].
PSO(Particle Swarm Optimization)is an evolution method proposed by Kennedy and eberhart[4]. It can obtain good performance in optimization problem. In this paper, we take Zhengya He’s modified Particle Swarm Optimization

algorithm[5] (HE_PSO) to optimize the girth distribution of parity check matrix.Furthermore the QC LDPC codes is constructed by the optimized matrix.
This paper is organized as follows. In section II, preliminary theory of QC LDPC codes is briefly reviewed. In Section III, proposed optimization scheme, GirthOpt-PSO algorithm is given. Simulation result and performance analysis are given in Section IV. Section V is the conclusion of this paper.

B. Girth Detection of QC LDPC Codes
The cycle properties QC LDPC codes are important to the performance of them. The girth is defined as the minimum length of cycles in the Tanner graph of LDPC codes. Girth distribution of H matrix has an important effect on the performance of LDPC codes.

To determine the girth distribution of S matrix ,the following theorm is given[1] ,

Theorm : A necessary and sufficient condition for the element s1ˈs2ˈ…….s2nin S matrix to form a short cycle is

s2-s1+s4-s3+.......+s2n-s2n-1 = 0 mod P

(4)

The procedure of girth detection for the S matrix is described as follows:

(1) scanning all the element in the S matrix ,calculating the sum according to equ.(4) and determine whether there is a short cycle in the matrix.

(2) Avoiding 4 girth and 6girth in the matrix will benefit to the performance of QC LDPC codes[1,2].When there is 4girth or 6girth is detected(4girth means the minimum short cycle length of the matrix is 4 ),the matrix would be abandoned, search procedure is started again. The matrix choosed as above is guaranteed no less than 8girth. The numbers of 8girth could be obtained by adding up the numbers when the sum is zero.

It should be noted that the S matrix is relatively small , scanning and detecting all the element is possible.

When the S matrix is obtained , H matrix can be constructed by equ.(1). The relation of cycle properties between S matrix and H matrix is described as following corollary:

Corollary : If the elements of S matrix s1, s2 , s3, s4 ,......, s2n form a short cycle, then there will be P short cycles in the corresponding H matrix(P is the rows or columns of submatrix ).

III. PROPOSED SCHEME
In this section , GirthOpt-PSO algorithm is proposed , We took the PSO optimization algorithm to search the good girth distribution of S matrix. Furthermore the H matrix can be constructed by the S matrix.
PSO(Particle Swarm Optimization)algorithm is described as follows, as far as each particle in the solution space is concerened, they will search individual optimization result and label it with pbest; then compare all the individual optimization result to get the global optimization result with the label f(gbest) and gbest is the particle which obtain the gloal optimization result. Furthermore is the ”accelerate” process in the algorithm, all the particle “moving” to the region specified by pbest and f(gbest).Random accelerated factor is introduced in “accelerate”.

HE modified as follows: no cumulate velocity is applied to adjust the position of particle[5]. It converged more rapidly than original PSO algorithm.

The goal of optimization is that the H matrix has the girth distribution as good as possible, that is to say ,the girth is as large as possible and the numbers of short cycles is as less as possible. The algorithm is briefed described as follows:

It should be noted that the cost function is the numbers of short cycles with length 8.

(1) Initialization: particle is randomly distributed in the solution space, here means the initialization of the shift values, which is the element of S matrix.

(2) Adjust the position of each particle, that means searching better shift values according to the searching procedure described in section B of section II.

(3) Compare the cost function(here is the numbers of 8girth cycles)of the particle and individual optimization value pbest;if p > pbest,update pbest; else pbest is unchanged.
(4) Compare the current global optimization result f(g) and the optimization result gbest labeled; if f(g)>f(gbest), update f(gbest);else take f(gbest) unchanged.

From Fig 1 ,we can see that the H matrix constructed by proposed GirthOpt-PSO algorithm is yield to the definition of QC LDPC codes, whose distribution of nonzero element is regularity. The column weight and row weight of H matrix is still 3 and 6.

To compare and evaluate the performance of QC LDPC codes constructed by the proposed scheme further. Minimum distance of the proposed QC LDPC codes are compared with the other two QC LDPC codes ,Tanner code and array code[2,3].

Minimum Distance is a very important parameter of QC LDPC codes to evaluate its performance. It is upper-bounded by (J+1)!. The column weight J is 3 in the simulation , the upper bounded of minimum distance is 24.

It should be noted that finding the minimum distance of LDPC codes is an NP-hard problem. It is difficult for giving the minimum distance accurately. Hu and Fossorier proposed ANC (approximately nearest codewords)algorithm to finding the approximately minimum distance of LDPC codes[6]. Performance of constructed LDPC codes could be evaluated by applying ANC algorithm.

V. CONCLUSIONS
In this paper , a novel algorithm, GirthOpt-PSO algorithm, is proposed to constructed QC LDPC codes. The algorithm optimized the girth distribution of S matrix. Furthermore H matrix can be constructed by the obtained S matrix.
Minimum distance and BER performance both show that the QC LDPC codes constructed by the GirthOpt-PSO algorithm is superior than Tanner code and array code.Besides the proposed scheme is more flexible than Tanner code and array code.
Whether the QC LDPC codes constructed by the proposed scheme are optimal needs to be further discussed.
