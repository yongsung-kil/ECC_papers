I. INTRODUCTION
WiMAX (Worldwide Inter-operability for Microwave Access) is a communication technology that is an implementation of providing wireless transmission of data using a variety of transmission modes. It can structure underpinning of fixed, portable and mobile data networks. The WiMAX system based on the emerging IEEE 802.16 standards. The technology enables long distance wireless connections with high speeds and can be used for a number of applications, including โlast mileโ broadband connections, hotspot and cellular backhaul, and high speed enterprise connectivity for businesses.
In recent years, Low-Density Parity-Check (LDPC) codes [1] become popular in communication system. As a result of LDPC codes can achieve near Shannon limit performance [2] and when the codeword sizes have to be large that can be has the advantage in performance. In many communication systems which like 802.16e, DVB-S2 and 802.11n usage LDPC codes in channel coding to do Forward Error Correction (FEC).
Hybrid automatic repeat request (HARQ) protocols in conventional ARQ combines with FEC (Forward Error Correction). In conventional ARQ, a coded packet is transmitted initially and if the receiver decoding packet fails, the retransmission request in the form of no acknowledgment (NACK) will feed back to the transmitter. HARQ can advance retransmission mechanisms. When the receiver decoding packet fails, the receiver reserves the useful bits to the next decoding.
In IEEE 802.16e, the definitions of the HARQ modes are defined the CC (Chase Combining) and IR (Incremental Redundancy). In this paper, we study the Reliability-Based Hybrid ARQ (RB-HARQ) [5] which is an attractive ARQ scheme with Low-Density Parity-Check (LDPC) codes. RB-

HARQ use the Soft-input, Soft-output (SISO) decoding scheme such as convolutional codes and turbo codes has been proposed [3][4][5]. Therefore, the Log-Likelihood Ratios (LLRs) of soft output was decoded by LDPC decoder and according to [3], the transmitter can retransmit the small reliability bits to the receiver and combine the bit to decoding. The other research [6][7][8], the research represents the bit reliability base on the degrees. So we analyze the relationship bteween bit reliability and degrees in this paper and combine the two methods to select the unreliability bits.
In Fig. 1 and Fig. 2 show the HARQ of Chase Combining and RB-HARQ. In Chase Combining, the receiver decoding error and transmit an NACK to request the retransmission again. In RB-HARQ, if the receiver decoding error, the receiver select the unreliability bits to request retransmission. The transmitter only transmits the unreliability bits to the receiver. The receiver combines the unreliability bits to increase redundancy.
