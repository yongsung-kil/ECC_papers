In the eigenmessage step, elements of a message along a cycle of edges ure expressed as a linear combination of that message itself, giving rise to an eigenequation, whose solution - the eigenmessage - is equioalent to the jixed point of the closed loop message passing algorithm, where the other edges incident o n nodes in the cycle are held fixed. The decoding algoiithm iterates among eigenmessage steps for the cycles in the graph and conventional message passing for edges not in cycles. The eigeizmessoge algorithm results iii faster convergence (approxim.ate1y 70 percent) .without any compromise in error performance compared with conventional message passing algorithms.
