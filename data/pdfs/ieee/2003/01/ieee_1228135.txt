I. INTRODUCTION
We have constructed new families of LDPC code ensembles which are sub-ensemble of conventional irregular LDPC code ensembles and developed density evolution for the new ensembles. Moreover, we show a natural ensemble forms a typical ensemble of its super ensemble and density evohition for the typical ensemble agrees with that of its super ensemble. Luby et al. have improved performance of LDPC codes with irregular graphs. Similarly, with this ensembles, we can obtain more flexibility in balancing requirements that variable nodes want high degrees and check nodes want low degrees.
