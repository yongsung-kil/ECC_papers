V. CONCLUSION
We have introduced a combinatorial construction of a class of high rate iteratively decodable codes using (v,k,l) BIBD. The resulting codes have column weight not less than three and minimum girth six. W e have constructed a balanced incomplete block design using Netto and Buratti cyclic difference families, as well as the novel affine geometry lattice designs, and showed that the encoding complexity is determined by the size of the difference family upon which the block design is based. We derived tight bounds on a minjmum distance of BIBD codes using a Pasch configuration argument.
