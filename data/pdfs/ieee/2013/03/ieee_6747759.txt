AbstractвАФIn

Log-
likelihood ratio (LLR) without channel state information (CSI)
is complicated. The maximum capacity linear approximation
(MCLA) has been proposed to reduce the computational com-

I. INTRODUCTION
L
DPC codes have achieved excellent performance over
additive white Gaussian noise (AWGN) channel. Appli-
cation of LDPC codes on Rayleigh fading channel has been
studied in [1],[2]. In [1], J. Hou et al. focused on irregular
LDPC code optimization. In [2], R. Yazdani et al. proposed
MCLA method to reduce the computational complexity of
LLR for LDPC decoding without CSI. The MCLA method
needs accurate probability density function (PDF) of LLR
which lead to complicated computations.
In this letter, we propose a simple Gaussian approximation
to PDF of LLR. The proposed approximation method only
needs the mean and variance of channel gain instead of
accurate PDF of LLR. The BER performance of the proposed
method is evaluated via computer simulations.

V. CONCLUSIONS
We have proposed a simple Gaussian approximation based
on MCLA method for LDPC code to reduce the computational
complexity of the PDF of LLR without CSI at receiver. Our
proposed method only needs the mean and variance of channel
gain, while MCLA method needs the PDF of LLR. Hence the
computational complexity of our proposed method is much
lower than the MCLA method. Furthermore, simulation results
have shown that the BER performance of our proposed method
is almost the same as the MCLA method.
