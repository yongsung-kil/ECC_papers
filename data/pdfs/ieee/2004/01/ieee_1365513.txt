Estimating LDPC Codeword Error Rates via Importance Sampling
Bo Xia and William E. Ryan Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering
University of Arizona, Tucson, AZ 85721, USA xia,ryan@ece.arizona.edu

Abstract — This paper proposes an importance sampling design for the codeword error rate estimation of low-density parity-check codes. An error set partitioning method is introduced to reduce the error boundary complexity and to improve the simulation efficiency. This is a follow-up to the work in [1].
