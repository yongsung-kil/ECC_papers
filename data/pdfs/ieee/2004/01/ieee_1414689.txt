Design Semi-Algebraic LDPC Codes on u/u+v Construction
Dong Min Lee and MoonHo Lee, Sarm Gu Cho Institute of Information & Communication, Chonbuk National University, Chonju, 561-756, Korea
KETI, MaSan-Ri, JinWi-Myon, PyungTaek-Si, KyungGi-Do 451-865, South Korea Email: dmlee74@hotmail.com, chosg@keti.re.kr

AbstractÔÇöIn this paper, semi-algebraic Lowdensity parity-check(SA-LDPC) codes on u/u+v construction are proposed. The codes by the u/u+v construction have the extended error capacity for additional Hamming weight. It is a simple way to construct a large size block code using smaller ones. Semi-algebraic low-density parity-check codes in this class have a large girth and good minimum distances. They perform well with iterative decoding.
Keywords-u/u+v codes;SA- LDPC; sum product.
I. INTRODUCTION
LDPC codes now have attracted a great deal of interest, since they can perform extremely close to the Shannon limit[9]. Therefore, many literatures presented the simple and efficient ways to construct LDPC codes by using permutation matrices, BICD, and quasi cyclic[4][5]. However, they all are limited by rate, code length and row weight etc. That is to say, it is difficult to design such code has same rate but different length, or same length but different rate, and so on. In this paper, we investigate a linear combination method based on u/u+v construction which can easily extend the rate or code length based on some simple or efficient LDPC designs. The u/u+v construction first introduced by [1,2] is a method of combing two linear block codes. Codes by the u/u+v construction have many remarkable properties, such as the powerful error correcting capabilities, simple encoding generators and multilevel decoding combinations. Therefore, some of the best block codes for error correction were found in them [1]. Recently, Luby et al. extended GallagerÔÇÖs results to consider irregular codes, that is, codes with nonconstant row and column weights in , and showed that these codes are capable of outperforming regular codes[8]. The irregular codes are constructed via a pseudo-random process which usually involves discarding codes which contain 4-cycles. In this paper, a semi-algebra method for constructing LDPC codes based on BCH codes is presented. Proposed code is one of deterministic LDPC codes based on the dualdiagonal sub-matrix. This pattern is similar in structure to[3]. The construction method results in a class of high rate LDPC codes. Codes in this class have a large girth and good minimum distances. They can perform

well with the iterative decoding. By combining this idea with LDPC codes design, in this paper, we now investigate the performances of the LDPC codes based on u/u+v construction. The simulation results demonstrate that the proposed construction can approach a very good performance.

V. CONCLUSION
In this paper, we proposed the u/u+v construction for error capacity for additional Hamming weight. It is an efficient way to extend SA-LDPC Codes to flexible length or rate with a better performance. A simple LDPC code construction base in semi-algebra method with girth at least 6 has been presented.
