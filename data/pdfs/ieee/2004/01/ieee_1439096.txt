I. INTRODUCTION
Recently, many researches have focused on low-density
parity-check (LOPC) codes which are considered serious
competitors to Turbo codes in terms of performance and
complexity. It is shown that the BP algorithm [1][2] provides
a powerful tool for iterative decoding of LDPC codes. Com­
paring with Turbo codes, the decodi ng complexity of LOPC
codes associated with BP algor ithm per information bit per iteration is evaluated based on the check-node and bit-node updates in [2] and [3], and it is reported the complexity of BP algorithm is much lower than Turbo codes. However, it is well
known that BP algorithm converges after several hundreds to
thousands iterations because of it's slow convergence speed
[4]. Since the overall decoding complexity depends on the
number of iterations, the overall complexity of BP alg orith m is slightly lower than Turbo codes at low SNR.
[n this paper, we propose a new schedule of BP algorithm of regular LOpe codes for a fast convergence. We apply BP algorithm over bipartite graph to a iterative message­ passing implementation on a directed acyclic graph (OAG) [5]. By using a Gauss-Seidel iteration algorithm which is a
asynchronous parallelization of the iterative message-passing implementation on a DAG, we group the check-nodes on the bipartite graph and schedule the updates of the group for optimum convergence speed. Therefore, the princ iple of the
proposed algorithm is the grouping and scheduling updates of
check-node message without additional computations or ap­ proximations. Thus, the proposed scheme can be implemented
for the decoder having serialized constructions. However, to
apply the proposed algorithm, analysis or rearrangement of
