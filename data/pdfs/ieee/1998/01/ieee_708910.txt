Abstract - We present a class of convolutional codes defined b y a low-density parity-check matrix, and an iterative algorithm for the decoding of these codes. The performance of t h i s decoding is close to the performance of turbo-decoding.
I. CODECONSTRUCTION
Motivated by the results of Gallager [l], systematically encoded rate R = 1/2 binary (M,J,K)-codes has been considered. They are convolutional codes with memory M and defined by a periodical and sparse parity-check matrix H with columns having Hamming weight W H = J and with rows having WH = K .
