ABSTRACT 
Researches indicate that the resistance of phase­
change material will become larger over the time. Use 
time-aware based soft-decision error correction code, 
which needs LLR to decode, can correct the errors in 
multilevel PCM effectively. Accurate LLR needs to read 
the resistance accurately, which will lead to a longer trans­
mission latency. In this paper, we proposed a non-uniform 
correction strategy, which can reduce the read levels 
maintaining bit-error-rate perfonnance. We use LOPC in 
correction of 4-level per cell PCM, and get the result via 
computer simulation. 
INTRODUCTION 
Phase-Change Memory (PCM) has become one of the 
today's biggest concerns due to its nonvolatile, multilevel 
storage, scalability, etc.. PCM is using chalcogenide 
material, usually Ge2Sb2TeS (GST). GST can be 
configured into either a crystalline or an amorphous phase, 
and resistances between the two phases differ by several 
orders of magnitude. Due to the vast difference in 
resistance, and the phase-change material can be 
programed into several different states between crystalline 
and amorphous phase [1], so PCM can store more bits in 
one cell, which improves the data access amount in PCM. 
However, some papers indicate that there are several 
problems in PCM such as read and write crosstalk, 
crystallization [2]. And as demonstrated in the studies 
[3-S], resistance of phase-change material may drift over 
time. We can find that the impact of problems besides 
resistance drift become smaller with the progress of 
technology [6][7].So the resistance drift is the most 
important problem of PCM and poses a severe reliability 
challenge, particularly for multi-level PCM. 
In recent study [8], it has proposed a time-aware 
memory sensing strategy to address resistance drift issue. 
In this paper, both hard-decision ECC and soft-decision 
ECC have been used. We can clearly find that the 
soft-decision ECC has stronger ability of error correction.

In our work, we have proposed a non-uniform 
strategy based on the time-aware sensing to fill such a 
missing link. First, we give hypothetical 4-bits/cell PCM 
based on the equipment [3]. Second, we calculate the LLR 
at time t. Third, we divide the distribution of IgR into 
different areas according to the entropy at time t. And 
different states in one area has the same LLR. We also do 
entropy coding for various areas to further reduce the 
transmission delay. 
We use the rate 9/10 QC-LOPC as a test vehicle. And 
using non-uniform correction in IS level error correction 
at the time of I day, the transmission efficiency increases 
by 2S%. And BER performance does not decrease.

CONCLUSION 
Phase change memory is one of the new memories in 
next generation. However, a serious problem that resis­
tance drift affecting the multi-level phase-change memory 
storage reliability, the use of soft-decision decoding can 
effectively improve the BER performance of PCM. But 
the soft decision ECC will increase transmission delay 
between memory and the memory controller. This paper 
presents a non-uniform error correction scheme to solve 
this problem. Matlab simulation of non-uniform error 
correction scheme in 4 level/cell PCM has been performed, 
the results shows it can reduce read levels with good BER 
performance.
