Efficient Min-Sum Decoding of LDPC Codes Based on Layer Schedules
Chaobo Liu and Xiaoyou Yu

AbstractвАФFlooding schedule (FS) and shuffled schedule (SS) are the mainstream Strategy applied in LDPC iterative decoding; however, FS need lots of Hardware devices for storage and SS has low computing speed. In Deep Space communication (DSC), these shortcomings will influence performance and practicability seriously. This paper presented an improved decoding algorithm which grouping base on the variable nodes. The simulation results show that the improved decoding performance enhance 0.3-0.5dB comparing with FS in bit-error-rate (BER) of 10-7 and the decoding speed is k multiple of SS in ka wave band, k is the number of group.

I. INTRODUCTION
ERROR correction coding is developing as one of decisive technique in Deep Space Communications (DSC). As one of error correction coding, LDPC codes offer excellent error correction performance [1]. Such as low decoding complexity, delay of time, good channel flexibility and so on. Because of the excellent performance, now LDPC codes were applied in communication system, digital signal processing and artificial intelligence fields [2]. In 2007, JPL has proposed LDPC codes in CCSDS. The draft has boosted the LDPC codes application in DSC [3].
Signal power and delay have been the bottle problems in DSC [4]. So itвАЩs practical to reduce the computing complexity and decoding times. This paper summarized the disadvantages and advantages of FS [5] and SS [12] from the decoding delay, complexity and decoding capability. The improved MS decoding grouping based on variable nodes inherit several merits of them and offers better performance to trade-offs. Comparing with SS and FS, the improve algorithm can get better astringency and need less memorizers. The remainder of this paper is organized as follows: Section II discusses the FS. Section III describes the SS. Section IV describes the improved method and shows the result of simulate. Section V concludes the paper.

III. SCHUFFLED SCHEDULE
Usually, SS has two kinds of methods to update. One is update on variable nodes , SS denotes the algorithm in table . The other is base on check nodes, SSrepresents it. The different between them is the order of updating. SS updates message in each row, SS in each line. In SS. The initial value of a new line or row is from lines or rows above them. The final line or row as the outcome in an iteration.
After every whole iteration of all variable nodes per row, every node updates asynchronously in SS. The probability correct-decoding increase with message updating;

Theoretical, a whole iteration in SS is equivalent to several whole iterations in FS. Figs display that the curve of average iteration in SS drop more sharply than FS. The result shows that SS need less iteration in an updating.

V. CONCLUSION
As one of three activities for space, the deep-space exploration program brought many countries attention on it in new century. DSC requires low complexity in equipment and good coding gains, so error-correcting become a necessary technique. In paper, the improved method is a based on variable nodes MS. the performance balances between SS and FS. The result of simulation proved that the improved method suit the DSC. The method we proposal is available and sensible for the research of low LDPC codes decoding.
