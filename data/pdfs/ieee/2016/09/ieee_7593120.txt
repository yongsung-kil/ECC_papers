Abstract-This paper deals with symbol-pair error channels. First, we generalize the channels to symbol-tuple error channels whose read length is D. Then, we derive a message passing algo­ rithm for decoding LDPC codes over the generalized channels. Furthermore, we investigate the Hamming distance distributions of LDPC codes in terms of D symbol-tuple metric.
I. INTRODUCTION
Cassuto and Blaum presented symbol-pair read channels and a coding framework for the channels. The outputs of the channels are overlapping pairs of symbols in high-density data storage applications. They defined the pair distance as a metric in the channels and showed the pair error correcting capability of codes over the channels. In [2], Cassuto and Litsyn also constructed cyclic symbol-pair codes using algebraic methods. On the other hand, Yaakobi et al. [6] and Takita et al. [4] proposed decoding algorithms for cyclic codes over the symbol-pair read channels. Furthermore, Yaakobi et al. [7] extended this decoding algorithm to b-symbol read channels. Additionally, Takita et al. [5] proposed a method for syndrome decoding of linear block codes over the symbol-pair read channels.
These code constructions and their decoding algorithms over symbol-pair read channels have been studied by using algebraic methods. On the other hand, LDPC codes execute good performance by iterative decoding algorithms over the binary symmetry channels and additive white Gaussian noise channels [8]. In [9], it has been proved that spatially-coupled LDPC codes achieve the channel capacity. These codes are studied in modern coding theory [14]. However, LDPC codes and decoding algorithms over the symbol-pair read channels have not yet been reported.
In this paper, we deal with symbol-pair error channels. First, we generalize the channels to symbol-tuple error channels whose read length is D. Then, we derive a message passing algorithm for decoding LDPC codes over the generalized channels. We also show the performance of LDPC codes by the proposed decoding algorithm. Furthermore, we investigate the Hanuning distance distributions of the LDPC codes in terms of D symbol-tuple metric.

VI. CONCLUSION

In this paper, we introduced a probabilistic model of symbol-tuple error channels. The channels are considered as a generalization of symbol-pair read channels. We have derived the message passing algorithm for decoding LDPC codes over the generalized channels. Furthermore, we investigate the Hamming distance distributions of the LDPC codes in terms of Dsymbol-tuple metric.Furthermore,we have shown the performance of LDPC codes by the proposed decoding algorithm. In future works, we will derive the capacity of symbol-tuple error channels and will compare the decoding performance of LDPCcodes with the capacity. Additionally, we will detailedly analyze the characterisation of Hamming distance distributions of the LDPCcodes over Dsymbol-tuple error channels.
