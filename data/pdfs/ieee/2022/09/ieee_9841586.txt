LDPC-CCs were first proposed in [22] 109 and characterized by the degree distributions of the under110 lying LDPC block codes (LDPC-BCs). In addition, spatially 111 coupled LDPC (SC-LDPC) codes are constructed by cou112 pling a number of LDPC-BCs. As the number of coupled 113 LDPC-BCs tends to infinity, spatially coupled LDPC convolu114 tional codes (SC-LDPC-CCs) are obtained. In [23] and [24], 115 SC-LDPC-CCs have been shown to achieve capacity over 116 binary memoryless symmetric channels under BP decoding. 117 In [25], SC-LDPC-CCs have been constructed from the per118 spective of protographs, namely SC-PLDPC-CCs. Through 119 the edge-spreading procedure on protomatrix, the threshold, 120 convergence behavior and error performance of SC-PLDPC 121 ensembles have also been systematically investigated. In 122 particular, PEXIT algorithms have been applied to analyze 123 binary [26] and q-ary SC-LDPC codes [27]. 742 group is first created or initialized and a fitness function is

743 developed to calculate the fitness value of each individual
