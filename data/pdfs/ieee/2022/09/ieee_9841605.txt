369 C. System and Network Model
370 We consider a blockchain system similar to [1] and [2] that 371 has full nodes and light nodes. One of the full nodes acts 372 as a block producer of a new block. We consider the same 373 blockchain network model as [2]. In particular, we assume 374 a synchronous network where the subgraph of honest full 375 nodes is connected7 and the messages sent on the network are 376 anonymous. The network can have a dishonest majority of full 377 nodes, but each light node is connected to at least one honest 378 full node (thus preventing eclipse attacks [1]). Nodes broadcast 379 a message (fraud proofs, IC proofs, and CMT symbols) by 380 sending the message to all its connected nodes.
