- **Published**: Sept.15, 2
- **Authors**: Deva K. Borah, David G. Voelz
- **PDF**: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/4914860
- **Abstract**: The effects of pointing errors on the performance of a free-space optical laser communication system are studied. The log-normal and gamma-gamma irradiance probability density function models have been considered to include the effects of optical turbulence. An expression for the mean intensity under pointing fluctuations is derived. Results for ergodic and outage channel capacities are presented.
