# IEEE Xplore — 1997-01 (1차선별 통과)


## Graph theoretic construction of generalized product codes

- **Status**: ✅
- **Reason**: 고지스 이분그래프 기반 일반화 곱부호+반복 MAP 복호; GLDPC 성격이나 high-girth 그래프 구성은 LDPC 코드설계(E)로 이식 가능성, 애매하여 보류
- **알고리즘 기여**: ✅ 알고리즘/코드 기여
- **ID**: ieee:613026
- **Type**: conference
- **Published**: 1997
- **Authors**: P. S. Guinand, J. H. Lodge
- **PDF**: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/613026
- **Abstract**: In this note we describe a construction, based on bipartite graphs of high girth, of codes which lend themselves to an iterative MAP decoding strategy.

## On the construction of efficient multilevel coded modulations

- **Status**: ✅
- **Reason**: 다중레벨 코디드 모듈레이션 다단 반복복호를 belief propagation으로 통합 설명, LDPC/LDGM 포함한 BP 메시지패싱 관점으로 이식 가능성, 애매하여 보류
- **알고리즘 기여**: ✅ 알고리즘/코드 기여
- **ID**: ieee:613459
- **Type**: conference
- **Published**: 1997
- **Authors**: Jung-Fu Cheng
- **PDF**: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/613459
- **Abstract**: The methodology of multi-stage iterative decoding with exchange of soft information has previously been applied to a wide range of error-controlling codes with remarkable success, including parallel/serial concatenated convolutional/block codes (Berrou et al., 1993) and generalized concatenated convolutional codes (Cheng). A theoretical explanation of this decoding mechanism has been proposed (MacKay et al.) via the belief propagation algorithm (BPA) (Pearl 1988). It is shown that, by representing a turbo code with a Bayesian belief network, the application of BPA directly leads to the turbo decoding algorithm. In fact, it is argued that the decoding algorithms for all the aforementioned variations of the turbo coding construction as well as a wide range of low-density parity check codes can be explained by this principle. The author goes on to discuss low density generator matrix codes.

## Good error-correcting codes based on very sparse matrices

- **Status**: ✅
- **Reason**: Gallager 바이너리 LDPC + belief propagation 복호의 정전적 논문, 핵심 LDPC 디코더(C)/부호(E)
- **알고리즘 기여**: ✅ 알고리즘/코드 기여
- **ID**: ieee:613028
- **Type**: conference
- **Published**: 1997
- **Authors**: D. J. C. MacKay
- **PDF**: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/613028
- **Abstract**: We report theoretical and empirical properties of Gallager's (1963) low density parity check codes on Gaussian channels. It can be proved that, given an optimal decoder, these codes asymptotically approach the Shannon limit. With a practical 'belief propagation' decoder, performance substantially better than that of standard convolutional and concatenated codes can be achieved; indeed the performance is almost as close to the Shannon limit as that of turbo codes.
